# Q-1: What are the new tags added in HTML5?

## Ans.

### In HTML5, two main types of tags were added: structural tags and semantic tags.

- **Structure Type**
    - `<header>`
    - `<nav>`
    - `<aside>`
    - `<article>`
    - `<section>`
    - `<footer>`

- **Semantic Tags**
    - `<abbr>`
    - `<address>`
    - `<br>`
    - `<button>`
    - `<canvas>`
    - `<caption>`
    - `<legend>`
    - `<ltr>`
    - `<rtl>`
    - `<fieldset>`
    - `<detail>`
    - `<pre>`
    - `<sup>`
    - `<sub>`
    - `<svg>`
    - `<video>`
    - `<audio>`
    - `<poster>`
    - `<blockquote>`
    - `<object>`
